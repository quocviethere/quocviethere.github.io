Divergence Measures

Given two distributions and , sometimes it is helpful to measure the difference between the two distributions, i.e. how "close" are they to each other. Here, we consider the divergence measure which quantifies how far is from , without requiring that be a metric [1].
There are many divergence measures, three of the most common are Kullback-Leibler Divergence, Jensen-Shannon Divergence, Wasserstein distance, and Total Variation.
